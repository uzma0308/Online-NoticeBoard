Online Notice Board
This app is a place where student can leave any types of messages and notices, for example, to advertise things, announce events or provide any information. The main aim of this fapp is to make information dissemination much easier in a paperless community. By the help of free online notice board student can access the notices and important information quickly not only in the particular premises, also wherever and whenever they need to know.
